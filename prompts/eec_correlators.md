# Energy-Energy Correlators and Charged Multiplicity -- ALEPH at sqrt(s) = 91.2 GeV

Type: measurement

````text
Measure the projected two-point energy-energy correlator (EEC), its
asymmetry (AEEC), and the mean charged particle multiplicity in
hadronic Z decays using archived ALEPH data at sqrt(s) = 91.2 GeV.

The EEC is the energy-weighted angular correlation of all particle
pairs per event. The AEEC has reduced hadronization corrections.
The mean charged multiplicity is a complementary validation observable
sharing the same tracking corrections. Use charged particles only
(pwflag == 0, highPurity == 1).

References:
- ALEPH EEC: CERN-PPE/92-113 and Eur. Phys. J. C35 (2004) 457
- PDG mean n_ch = 20.76 +/- 0.16 at sqrt(s) = 91.2 GeV
- Query the LEP corpus for ALEPH EEC and charged multiplicity
````
